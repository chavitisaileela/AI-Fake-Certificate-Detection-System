About LeelaX AI

Securing credential integrity with automated optical forensics and deep learning document verification.

Our Mission

In an increasingly digital academic and professional landscape, document forgery has become more sophisticated than ever. Welcome to LeelaX AI, an advanced AI-driven verification engine built to validate credentials instantly.

We specialized in automated document screening. By running uploaded assets through layout analysis, optical character checking, and pattern recognition models, LeelaX AI accurately diagnoses structural elements to confirm whether they are genuine or fraudulent.

Instead of manual verification, our system calculates a precise authenticity percentage score, providing organizations, HR structures, and academic institutions with a definitive metric on credential reliability.
